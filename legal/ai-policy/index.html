AI Policy

The following policy applies to any programmed / automated requests for information made to any of the pages on this website.

What is Considered "Automated"?

By "programmed" or "automated" services, we are refering to any of the following (a non-exhaustive list):

  • Artificial Inteligence (AI) - generative or otherwise (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude etc)
  • Get/Fetch/Post or other types of requests made by programming languages (Python, JavaScript etc)
  • Web Crawlers
  • Requests made by any third-party automation services
  • Copying the data "by-hand" for AI or automation training

Unauthorised Automation

Access to this website via unauthorised automation is prohibited.

Authorised Automation

If you want to gain access to the contents of this website for automaion purposes (such as training a generative AI model), contact us.

The following conditions apply:

Available Data

Only the content within the blog section of this website (mainly posts) is available for Automation use.

Access To Data

Contact us to get access to raw versions of the data. Only two file formats are available: markdown (.md) or plain text (.txt).

We request that you do not make get/fetch or other automated requests directly to the page of interest to get access to the offered data. We can discuss this further to suite your needs.

Changes to AI Policy

We may update this policy at anytime without prior warning. We will not individually notify you of such changes. Therefore, it is your responsibility to regularly review these policies for any changes.

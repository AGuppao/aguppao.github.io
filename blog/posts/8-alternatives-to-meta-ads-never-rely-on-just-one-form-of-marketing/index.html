8 Alternatives to Meta Ads: Never Rely on Just One Form of Marketing

Abhay Gupta

Nov 17, 2024

Imagine that you're going on a well-deserved holiday after scaling your business.

You get to the airport and see your private jet waiting for you.

You get on and as you're about to depart, the pilot makes an announcement:

"Just a heads up, folks — we'll be flying with only one engine today since the other one's non-functioning."

Would you continue with the flight?

Probably, not. Right?

So, what does this analogy have to do with marketing?


One is the Worst Number in Business

Why?

Because you're dead in the water if that 'one' is taken away.

It's like that jet analogy from before - if that one engine fails, it's going to cause some problems.

And what makes your business any different?

Think about it for a second...

One key member of staff.

One big client.

One source of leads.

What happens if that 'one' is taken away?

The 'best case' scenario is that your business struggles to grow a bit. Maybe you lose a few clients.

And the 'worst case'... let's try to avoid this scenario.

Now, there are many ways this rule can be applied to your business.

But, I won't go into all of them here. Let's focus on marketing.


Why this Rule Matters in Marketing

You're trying to grow your business, right?

Well, you can't rely on just one form of marketing.

Because, if something happens to that 'one' method...

You're going to struggle to grow your business.

I mean, it's not exactly the end of the world... but it's not ideal.

Let's take Meta Ads as an example. What could go wrong with Meta Ads?

Your account gets blocked. Meta's servers go down. Social media gets banned by the government...

It doesn't really matter what could happen. What matters is what you can do about it.

So, what should you be doing to prevent the 'ones' from causing problems for your marketing?


8 Alternatives to Meta Ads to Use Alongside Meta Ads

Are your Meta Ads working?

Great, then let's keep finding more ways to push that ad to more people.

And if Meta Ads didn't work for you, then let's try something else.

I mean, we are spoiled for options:

  • Google Ads

  • Email Marketing

  • Content Marketing

  • Affiliate Marketing

  • Event Marketing

  • Print Advertising

  • Direct Mail

  • Telemarketing

Now, they all have their own advantages and disadvantages. And they do depend on what industry you're in.

But they all work. Well... as long as you're following the basic principles for advertising.


Meta Ads are awesome. But they can go wrong. Whether it's account restrictions or Meta's servers going down.

So, don't rely on just Meta Ads. Try Google Ads, Email Marketing, Content Marketing or anything else.

And you're not restricted to just one or two forms of marketing. You can use as many as you want.

After all, 'one' is the worst number in business.

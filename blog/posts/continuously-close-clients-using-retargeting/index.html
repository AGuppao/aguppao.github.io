Continuously Close Clients Using Retargeting

Abhay Gupta

Sep 29, 2024

Have you ever had the experience of a product chasing you everywhere?

You check something out online. Be it a coat, a lawnmower or a car. Then, before you know it, every time you go on the Internet, you see a plethora of ads for that product for a week straight.

This is called 'retargeting' and it is another very powerful marketing technique.

But it's almost never used properly... at least by smaller businesses.

So, how does retargeting work and how can you use it to continuously land more clients?


Many Businesses Ignore this Simple Fact

Customers don't buy from you out of impulse. They go through a phase before they decide to buy anything.

This 'phase' is called the customer journey and I've already talked about it in a previous blog.

If you can reach your customers earlier in this 'journey', then they are more likely to buy from you.

I've already discussed how the customer journey stuff can be used in 2-Step Lead Generation (2SLG) to attract new clients.

But there's a small problem. People are forgetful.

By 'forgetful', I really mean that prospects might not be ready to buy from you then and there. And in doing so, they 'forget' about your company.

The reasons don't matter too much. What matters is that 'now' is not the time. So you need to 'remind' them later to take action.

So how can you 'remind' prospects to buy from you without nagging them with a river of emails?


Retargeting Prospects for Infinite Sales

Retargeting is essentially you frequently checking up on a prospect, like: "Hey, did you ever fix that problem? Do you want help with it now?".

You're simply reminding the prospect that you can still solve their problem.

And if they haven't already solved it and they're ready to fix it now, then you're already right in front of them.

And this is why retargeting is so amazing. Especially for smaller businesses.

You've basically cut past the competition by always being there for your prospects.

"So... how exactly does retargeting differ from 2SLG?"


2-Step Lead Generation vs Retargeting

2SLG and retargeting sound very similar.

That's because they both rely on the same basic idea - only sell to people who are actually interested in your offer.

They both do this by finding people who have interacted with your company in some way.

The main differences between the two are their specific use cases and how they are used to determine who is interested in your company.

For example, 2SLG involves a user showing interest in some of your content (like subscribing to a newsletter) and then you follow up with them (sales call / email).

Here you'd use something like an email list or a CTA to find out who is interested in your content.

Retargeting uses cookies to display relevant ads to users who have engaged with your company in some way (like visiting your website).

The neat thing about this, it's pretty much fully automated by default.

So you can focus on the other important aspects of your business.

Actually, this Cookie thing is huge. I'll spare the gory details for a separate blog.


In the meantime, start using retargeting to land even more clients by regularly reminding prospects that you have solutions to their problems.

Also, be sure to only target prospects who have actually shown interest in some way.
